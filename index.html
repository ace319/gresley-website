About

Who Is GRESLEY?

GRESLEY is the solo project of George Smith, a British singer, guitarist, pianist, and songwriter. He first became known as a member of New Hope Club, alongside Blake Richardson and Reece Bibby.

New Hope Club built a loyal fanbase through pop songs, harmonies, touring, and online content. After the group broke up at the end of 2024, George began a new chapter as a solo artist.

He chose the name GRESLEY because Gresley is his middle name, giving the solo project a personal connection to his identity.
