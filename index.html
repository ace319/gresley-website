Who Is George Smith?

George Smith is a British singer, guitarist, pianist, and songwriter best known as a member of New Hope Club. Alongside Blake Richardson and Reece Bibby, George helped build New Hope Club into a fan-loved pop band known for bright melodies, harmonies, and heartfelt songs.

George now releases solo music under the name GRESLEY. His solo work shows a more personal and emotional side of his songwriting.

George Smith and New Hope Club

New Hope Club became known for their pop and pop-rock sound, acoustic-driven performances, and strong connection with fans. George contributed vocals, guitar, and piano to the band.
