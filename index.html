About

Who Is George Smith?

George Smith is a British singer, guitarist, pianist, and songwriter. He first became known as a member of New Hope Club, the British pop band made up of George Smith, Blake Richardson, and Reece Bibby.

New Hope Club built a loyal fanbase through their pop songs, harmonies, touring, and online content. After New Hope Club broke up at the end of 2024, George began a new chapter as a solo artist under the name GRESLEY.

As GRESLEY, George’s music feels more personal, emotional, and intimate, centered around heartbreak, growth, and starting over.
