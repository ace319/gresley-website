Who Is George Smith?

George Smith is a British singer, guitarist, pianist, and songwriter best known as one third of the pop band New Hope Club. Alongside Blake Richardson and Reece Bibby, George helped build New Hope Club into a fan-loved pop group known for bright melodies, strong harmonies, and heartfelt songwriting.

After New Hope Club went on hiatus, George began a new chapter as a solo artist under the name GRESLEY. His solo music shows a more personal, emotional, and reflective side of his songwriting.

George Smith and New Hope Club

New Hope Club formed in 2015 and became known for their pop-rock sound, acoustic-driven performances, and close connection with fans. George contributed vocals, guitar, and piano to the band.

  • Band members: George Smith, Blake Richardson, and Reece Bibby
  • Formed in 2015
  • Known for pop and pop-rock music
  • Released the debut album New Hope Club in 2020

The GRESLEY Era

As GRESLEY, George Smith has stepped into a more vulnerable solo identity. His songs focus on growing up, heartbreak, change, distance, love, and finding himself after years in a band.

The GRESLEY project feels more intimate and personal, giving fans a closer look at George as both a songwriter and an artist.

Music Style

GRESLEY’s music blends emotional pop songwriting with acoustic textures, personal lyrics, and a softer singer-songwriter feel. While New Hope Club leaned into polished pop-rock energy, GRESLEY gives George room to tell his own story in a more honest and direct way.

  • Emotional songwriting
  • Acoustic and pop influences
  • Personal lyrics
  • A more mature solo sound
